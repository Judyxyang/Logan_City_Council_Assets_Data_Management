How This Demo Maps to the Role Requirements
📋 Role Requirement → Demo Evidence
Responsibility
Strategic WSUD advice & technical expertise — The WSUD Schema demonstrates deep knowledge of WSUD design parameters, filter media, macrophyte zones, hydraulic retention times and WQ targets for every treatment type.
Responsibility
Whole-of-life WSUD asset management — The Analyser covers the full lifecycle: construction year → condition grading → maintenance priority → remediation cost → budget forecast over 10 years. Open the Maintenance Priority and Budget Planning tabs.
Key Indicator
Stormwater quality offsets & frameworks — The Analyser → Offset Compliance tab tracks Active, Pending, Lapsed and Completed offset obligations by feature class with a lapsed offset action register. This directly reflects LCC's Stormwater Quality Offset Framework.
Key Indicator
Stormwater quality modelling & GIS — The Analyser → WQ Performance tab shows TSS/TP/TN compliance rates per treatment class. The Asset Map tab shows all 170 assets on OpenStreetMap with condition-grade colour coding and offset-status filtering.
Skill
Operational reporting & budget planning — The Analyser → Budget Planning tab models 10-year remediation cost under three intervention scenarios (Conservative/Moderate/Proactive) with annual and cumulative cost tables. Ready for direct use in management reporting.
Skill
Data quality & technical report writing — The Analyser → Data Quality tab audits all 170 records for missing IDs, out-of-range grades, logical errors and overdue maintenance, producing a severity-ranked issue register exportable to CSV for reporting.
Responsibility
Catchment load analysis & future planning — The Analyser → Catchment Analysis tab aggregates catchment area, WQ compliance rate and WSUD asset density by Logan suburb — directly supporting future infrastructure planning and community engagement reporting.
